## 1. Executive Summary
Circle is a financial technology company building infrastructure for the internet economy using stablecoins and blockchain technology. Its flagship product, USDC, is a regulated digital dollar stablecoin backed by cash and US Treasuries, enabling fast global payments and DeFi integration. Circle went public via IPO on NYSE: CRCL and offers developer tools, payments networks, and tokenized funds.

## 2. Company Overview
**사업 모델:** Circle generates revenue primarily from interest earned on reserves backing USDC and other stablecoins, transaction and redemption fees via Circle Mint, and service fees from APIs, liquidity, payments, and developer tools for businesses and institutions.

**핵심 제품/서비스:**
- USDC (USD Coin stablecoin)
- Circle Payments Network (global stablecoin payments)

## 6. Investment Thesis

**Bull Case:**
- Dominant USDC position as leading regulated stablecoin with institutional adoption and $30B+ circulation.
- Revenue growth from interest on reserves and expanding services like payments and developer tools amid crypto bull market.
- Public company status enables capital access for innovation in tokenized assets and global payments infrastructure.

**Bear Case:**
- High dependency on volatile crypto markets and USDC circulation which can contract sharply in bear markets.
- Regulatory risks from evolving global stablecoin rules could impose restrictions or compliance costs.
- Competition from Tether's larger USDT market share and emerging CBDCs threatening private stablecoins.
